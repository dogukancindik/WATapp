Visa Interview Questions

Common Visa Interview Questions

1. Why do you want to go to the United States?

To experience American culture, improve my English, and gain international work experience.

2. Have you traveled abroad before?

Yes/No. If yes, provide a brief explanation.

3. Why did you choose the Work and Travel program?

This program allows me to work and travel, explore new cultures, and build an international network.

4. How will you fund your trip?

I have savings and financial support from my family to cover all expenses.

5. Where will you stay in the United States?

I have arranged accommodation through the program or will stay at a pre-arranged location.

6. What will you do after returning from the United States?

I will continue my studies and share my experiences with peers.

7. How does this program align with your career goals?

This program will help me gain international experience and improve my skills, which are essential for my future career.
